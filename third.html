HTML LISTS

There are three types of lists in HTML:-

1. Ordered List

  1. coffee
  2. milk
  3. sugar

This is a ordered list in which numbring appears at first.

2. Unordered List

  • coffee
  • milk
  • sugar

This is an unordered list in whic a dot appears in front of the list

3. Discription List

milk
milk contains several neutrients in it.
coffee
coffee contains cafine in it.
sugar
sugar is made up of sugarcane.

Discription list gives a short discription about anything.
